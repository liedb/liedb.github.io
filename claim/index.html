False Claims

  • 122 Prisoners Released By Obama Administration from Guantanamo Returned to Battlefield
    Reality: 113 of them were released by President George W. Bush.
  • 2016 Election Biggest Electoral College Victory Since Reagan
    Reality: George H.W. Bush, Bill Clinton and Barack Obama all won bigger margins in the Electoral College.
  • 6 Block Lines for Trump's 2016 CPAC Speech
    Reality: There was no evidence of long lines.
  • ACA Caused Millions to Lose their Insurance
    Reality: The real number is less than 1 million, according to the Urban Institute.
  • Airport Delays Caused by Delta Outage Not Travel Ban
    Reality: The Delta outage happened two days later.
  • CNN's Ratings Are Way Down
    Reality: They're way up.
  • Chris Cuomo Didn't Ask Sen. Blumenthal About Vietnam
    Reality: It was part of Cuomo's first question.
  • Clinton Gave 20% of America's Uranium to Russia
    Reality: The State Department was one of nine agencies which had to approve the deal, it's unlikely Clinton was personally involved, and there is no evidence of a payment to Clinton.
  • Democrats Blocking Miners' Healthcare
    Reality: The bill to extend health benefits for certain coal miners was introduced by a Democrat and was co-sponsored by mostly Democrats.
  • Democrats Not Approving Trump Cabinet
    Reality: It’s hard to overstate what nonsense this is.
  • Democrats Obstructing Trump Nominees
    Reality: Mr. Trump had yet to nominate anyone for 478 out of more than 533 crucial appointments…
  • DoJ Submitted Watered Down Version of Travel Ban To Supreme Court
    Reality: Trump signed this version of the travel ban, not the Justice Department.
  • Half of Insurers Have Stopped Participating In Obamacare Exchanges
    Reality: Many fewer pulled out.
  • Half of MS-13 Deported
    Reality: The real number is in the hundreds, of approximately 10,000 members.
  • Half of Tennessee Has No Obamacare Insurer
    Reality: There's at least one insurer in every Tennessee county.
  • Highest Murder Rate in 47 Years
    Reality: It was higher in the 1980s and '90s.
  • House Healthcare Bill Protects Those With Pre-Existing Conditions As Well As Obamacare
    Reality: The bill weakens protections for people with pre-existing conditions.
  • I.C.E. Endorsed Trump
    Reality: Only its union did.
  • James Clapper Confirmed There is No Evidence of Russia/Trump Collusion
    Reality: Clapper only said he wasn't aware of an investigation.
  • London Mayor Claimed Terror Attack No Reason To Be Alarmed
    Reality: The mayor was specifically talking about the enlarged police presence on the streets.
  • Media Didn't Cover Large Crowds for Trump in Florida
    Reality: The media did cover it.
  • Mosul Campaign Expected to Take One Week
    Reality: The campaign was expected to take months.
  • N.A.T.O. Doesn't Cover Terrorism
    Reality: It has fought terrorism since the 1980s.
  • New York Times Apologized to Trump for Negative Coverage
    Reality: It never apologized.
  • Nobody Mentioned Clinton Receiving Debate Questions
    Reality: It was widely covered.
  • Obama Policies Created ISIS
    Reality: The group’s origins date to 2004.
  • Obama Wiretapped Trump During 2016 Campaign
    Reality: There's no evidence of a wiretap.
  • Obamacare Covers Very Few People
    Reality: About 20 million have gained insurance under the act.
  • Only 109 People Affected by Travel Ban
    Reality: At least 746 people were detained and processed…
  • Only Reporters Care About Trump's Tax Returns
    Reality: A majority of Americans do.
  • Paris Climate Agreement Binding
    Reality: The Paris climate agreement is nonbinding
  • Paris Climate Agreement Didn't Allow the U.S. To Build Coal Power Plants
    Reality: The agreement never uses the word coal, much less spells out how many coal-fired plants any country can build.
  • Pew Report Showed 2016 Voter Fraud
    Reality: The report never mentioned voter fraud.
  • Refugees Can't Be Vetted
    Reality: Refugees receive multiple background checks, taking up to two years.
  • Sen. Blumenthal Misrepresented Niel Gorsuch
    Reality: The Gorsuch comments were later corroborated.
  • Senate Healthcare Bill Increases Medicaid Spending
    Reality: The Senate bill significantly reduces how much the federal government would spend on Medicaid in future years compared to projections without a change in the current law.
  • Sweden Had Riots and Deaths the Day After Trump Falsely Implied There Had Been a Terrorist Attack
    Reality: Riots in Sweden broke out two days later and there were no deaths.
  • Terror Attack in Sweden
    Reality: Trump implied there was a terror attack in Sweden, but there was no such attack.
  • Terrorism Not Being Reported On
    Reality: Terrorism has been reported on, often in detail.
  • The New York Times Removed The Word “Wiretapped” From a Headline
    Reality: There were two different headlines on the online and print versions, neither of which were altered.
  • Travel Ban Rollout Was Smooth
    Reality: The rollout was chaotic.
  • Trump Didn't Change His Stance on China
    Reality: He did.
  • Trump Didn't Know Steve Bannon Until Late in His Campaign
    Reality: They Met in 2011
  • Trump Got China to Stop Manipulating Their Currency
    Reality: China stopped years ago.
  • Trump Got NATO Partners to Increase Defense Spending
    Reality: NATO countries agreed to meet defense spending requirements in 2014.
  • Trump Hadn't Heard of Wikileaks Before 2016 Campaign
    Reality: He criticized it as early as 2010.
  • Trump Inauguration Crowd Biggest Ever
    Reality: Official aerial photos show Obama's 2009 inauguration was much more heavily attended.
  • Trump Instituted a 5-year Ban on Lobbying the Executive Branch
    Reality: They can't lobby their former agency but can still become lobbyists.
  • Trump Opposed Invading Iraq
    Reality: He supported the invasion until after it began.
  • Trump Protesters Were Paid
    Reality: There is no evidence of paid protesters.
  • Trump Received 84% of the Cuban-American Vote in 2016
    Reality: There is no support for this.
  • Trump Responsible For 10,000 Walmart Jobs
    Reality: The jobs are a result of its investment plans announced in October 2016.
  • Trump Responsible for New Michigan Plants
    Reality: These investments were already planned.
  • Trump Saved Hundreds of Millions on F-35
    Reality: Most of the cuts were already planned.
  • Trump Unaware Flynn Discussed Sanctions With Russia
    Reality: Trump knew about Flynn's actions for weeks.
  • Trump Was on the Cover of Time Magazine More Than Anyone Else
    Reality: Trump was on the cover 11 times and Nixon appeared 55 times.
  • Trump's Appearance on “Late Show” Highest Rating Ever For Colbert
    Reality: Colbert's “Late Show” debut had nearly two million more viewers.
  • Trump's Trip Saved Billions of Dollars and Millions of Jobs
    Reality: Trump is counting his chickens before they hatch. Even if he succeeds in reorienting U.S. trade policy, jobs gains are still likely to be illusionary.
  • Trump/Russia Collusion Story Made Up By Democrats As Excuse For Losing 2016 Election
    Reality: The F.B.I. was investigating before the election.
  • Two People Were Killed During an Obama Speech
    Reality: There were no gun homicide victims in Chicago that day.
  • U.S. Has Highest Tax Rate
    Reality: It doesn't.
  • US Has $7 Billion Trade Deficit with Canada
    Reality: The U.S. had an $8.1 billion trade surplus, not deficit, with Canada in 2016.
  • US Producing As Much Steel as Ever, Just With Fewer Workers
    Reality: Production is down by one third, employment one fifth
  • Voter Fraud in 2016 Election
    Reality: There's no evidence of illegal voting.
  • Waters of the United States Rule Costing Hundreds of Thousands of Jobs
    Reality: There's no evidence that the Waters of the United States rule caused severe job losses.
  • Wikileaks Information Unclassified
    Reality: Not always. They have released classified information in the past.
